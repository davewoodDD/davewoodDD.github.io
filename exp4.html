Rotary Steerable Systems

Schlumberger PowerDrive & Exceed RSS

Schlumberger Nigeria , 2000 – 2007



ROTARY STEERABLE HORIZONTAL - highly experienced +5 yrs , Schlumberger operations Nigeria

Extensive drilling hours with Schlumberger Powerdrive & Exceed , Rotary Steerable Systems

Extensive horizontal wells with Rotary Steerable Systems

Offshore Escravos Nigeria , Roy Butler , Lloyd Noble , Key Bermuda , & Warri swamps Drillship Parker 74



Top hole motors if new drill then 8 1/2" & 6" lateral with RSS

50% redrills setting whipstock and sidetracking with RSS





Year 2000 offshore work was on 4 well jackets & mostly tangent wells with motors

Circa 2001 all development became horizontal , new wells or re drills of old producers often with pilot hole
to determine lateral target

Horizontal development had new challenges previously easy to drill and fast ROP wells at 45 -55 deg inclination
now became a significant drilling challenge due to hole conditions & problem shales & for motors ;

Stick- Slip ; PDC bits due to weight hanging & stick slip would not provide efficient sliding over 67 deg inclination

Experimented with everything , steering wheel bits , rockyback rotating near bit stabs , agitators and shock subs , and all these combined , high speed motors , slow speed motors . Sliding ROP would always fail with the PDC bit & with motors the new horizontal developments required a trip out of hole to change to an insert bit enabling the BHA to slide for final horizontal build & landing. Same problems for 6" lateral , needed insert bits to slide .



All work was now horizontal & the client opted for use of RSS

Initially the pads orientated Powerdrive had some mechanical issues & I’ve seen RSS do some strange things

After a period second generation Powerdrives were working better & the Point the bit Exceed was an excellent upgrade with significantly improved telemetry



Been a while since used RSS as the later work in Thailand was all motors & Ajustable Gauge Stabilisation

However in my distant past lies 8 years of MWD/LWD so all that telemetry , tool testing & set up is in my veins , if needed

Running RSS , pretty straightforward and those were the early tools
