Nigeria DD Operations

4-5 week rotation

Schlumberger Nigeria

7 years in Nigeria for the same operator Chevron based from the Escravos gas terminal , 2000 - 2007

Started Schlumberger as DD2 promoted DD3 & DDS senior directional driller in 2005



Early rotation was the drillship Parker 74 in the Warri swamps until security issues circa 2003 closed the Warri Delta field

Thereafter on the offshore jack-ups Key Bermuda Lloyd Noble & Roy Butler



Early work was tangent wells and drilled fast particularly on the swamp drillship

Circa 2001 most wells were planned as horizontal development & due to problematic hole conditions & shales at high angle there were some tough trips with tight hole & packing when tripping , & stick slip slow sliding at over 67 deg inclination with Motors & PDC bits

Rotary Steerable Systems were then introduced as the standard directional drilling tool for horizontal wells
