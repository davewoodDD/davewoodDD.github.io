Production line - batch drilling

Gulf of Thailand 16 wells on a 24 slot jacket





EVERY TOP HOLE 12 1/4" SECTION DRILLED IN 1 HOUR (max 1.5 hr)

& 3 HOURS LATER DRILLING THE NEXT ONE



8 1/2" HOLE - DRILLING A NEW ONE - EVERY DAY



Top Hole circa 1085 ft 9 5/8” csg point , inclinations 6 – 40 deg , next top hole 3 hours later , casing run fast & offline cement jobs ,

Under instruction to make hole with control with anti-collision & well placement critical same as any platform


After top hole batch drilling of 8 ½” hole circa 6775 ft 7” csg point , drilling in 8 hours or 10 hours if its a pregnant lady

Aiming for 550 ft/hr ROP's , drilling a new 8 ½” hole every day is busy & your next shift is always a different 8 1/2" well

In 16 days will drill 16 x 8 1/2 in hole sections & you gotta love the paperwork



& to finish every your fastest 8 1/2 in hole YOU NEED a CRITICAL line up on azimuth within 2.0 deg - that's on the ball every day x16

Every day in Gulf of Thailand is a normal DD Work Day on speed x 4 plus

Even for any seasoned DD this is exceptional and there is a learning curve but at the end of it


YOU ARE BETTER THAN YOU HAVE EVER BEEN


12 ¼" & 8 ½" with motors & hole conditions are good

6 1/8" hole (hot hole) is Rotary Assembly with Adjustable Gauage Stabiliser for inclination control

Very limited azimuthal control in 6 1/8" hole thus azimuth line up is critical on 8 1/2"



& A lot of analysis goes into 6 1/8" rotary sections with the DSV & Operator's Drilling Engineer , it’s generally going to walk left 70% of the time , careful planning on bit selection with options for left , straight , right trending bits , BHA selections are depending on hole inclination & options for near bit extensions have to be decided to maximise inclination control with the adjustable gauge stabiliser. Once quadrants are drilled around the platform & the walk trends are revealed rotary BHA selections are more successful

Cannot miss the targets , walk rates are carefully managed & if required there is the option to trip and make a short motor correction (hot hole) , or change the bit to right walking & 90 % of the time can make targets without a correction trip
