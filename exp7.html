Texas - Halliburton



My first JOB'S as DD - land work in Texas initially went for a DD training trip but happily ended up working rotation out of Houston

13 Nov 1996 through to 17 Jun 1997 & that was direct from a Texas rigsite to Hassi Messaoud Algeria , nice to be needed





Mostly well after well on the same rig

Not many days off , if your job finished then on another rig the next day , busy (plus Houston were paying Aberdeen to use me- they were happy)



In Texas if you’re a DD the small time operated land rigs expect a jack of all trades drilling consultant so you learn fast

These rigs are short handed and run on a shoestring


& that’s your job – Jack of all Trades Drilling consultant


& drilling with water you gonna spend all your time on the rig floor keeping things outta trouble

All insert & rock bits , drilling with water - makes a Toolface set an ART , problem shales , many bit trips , expect nothing but tight hole & packing in & out


It’s Great – Hands on everything & it’s every bit your job to keep the rig outta trouble
