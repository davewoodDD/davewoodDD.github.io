Algeria DD Operations

Algeria - Halliburton



Two years land work in the Sahara for Sonatrach & Groupement

Long trips did the whole well start to finish up to 2 months a trip (even longer sometimes if the rig broke down) it took an age to move the rig so time off was fine



Earlier tangent wells were easier to drill however the Groupement work was a horizontal development in the TFT gas field

Horizontal drilling led to challenges , hole conditon due to problem shales at high angle & frequent mud losses in sands but all wells completed successfully

Expected normally a week or longer on well control , (heavy mud) sandstone losses & conditioning problem shales

All drilling with insert bits & cautions on bit hours & watching the torque gauge carefully although most bits would go heavily undergauge in abrasive sandstones before cone issues & initially quite a bit of experimentation to find what bits work best

The DD is on the rig floor all day not just sliding but to keep a full time check to avoid drilling problems

Pretty knackering jobs 2 months on the rig floor in the desert is tough , i recall the well control issues were a wonderful break
